Privacy and Terms

1. PRIVACY AND DATA POLICY

1.1. Purpose and Scope +

This Privacy and Data Policy explains how Triptime AI ("platform," "we," or "project") handles and protects user data. Triptime AI provides AI-powered travel recommendations and trip list management services. The platform currently does not require official membership or payments. User data is stored only on their own device.

1.2. Collected Data and Sources +

Trip lists and user preferences are stored locally on the user's device. Recommendations are generated using the llama 3.2:1b model and open-source software when possible. Location data is optional; users can activate it to see their position on the map or leave it off. Cookies or tracking are minimal and only used if necessary for platform functionality.

1.3. Purposes of Data Usage +

Data is used solely to provide travel planning features and AI-generated recommendations. User data is never shared with third parties or transferred externally.

1.4. Data Security +

All user data is stored on their device and cannot be accessed by the platform. Users can view, edit, or delete their data at any time.

1.5. Policy Changes +

This policy may be updated due to platform improvements or legal requirements. Changes will be announced on the website and take effect on the date published.

2. TERMS OF USE

2.1. Introduction and Definitions +

These Terms govern the use of Triptime AI's AI-powered travel planning services. Users plan trips according to their own preferences. Official membership or payment systems are not required at this time.

2.2. Service Description +

Triptime AI allows users to create and visualize travel plans based on destination and duration, using a local trip list stored on the user's device.

2.3. Membership and User Data +

The platform is free to use. Users manage their trip lists locally and can freely create and edit their travel plans.

2.4. User Responsibilities +

Users are responsible for how they use the platform and manage their own data. The platform does not interfere with or access user data.

The platform provides AI-generated recommendations. Users are fully responsible for planning and managing their trips.
